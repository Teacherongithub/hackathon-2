# Chapter 1: Introduction to Robotics

Welcome to the **Mastering ROS 2** eBook. Robotics is the intersection of mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science.

## What You Will Learn
1. How to set up a robotic workspace.
2. The core architecture of modern robots.
3. How to use ROS 2 as the "brain" of your system.

> **Goal:** By the end of this chapter, you will understand the basic hardware-software loop of a robot.
